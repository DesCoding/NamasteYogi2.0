NamasteYogi
Meditation Yoga
  • Meditation Instructions:
  • Unmute the the song below.
  • Set a timer to hold space for the duration of practice.
  • Find a comfortable position.
  • Bring your attention to your breath (option to close eyes).
  • Inhale, feel your belly rise and ribs expand.
  • Exhale, fully and completely, allow your lungs to empty.
  • Continue this deep belly breathing as your awareness shifts to the song.
  • Each time your mind wanders, bring your focus back to your breath and the song.
  • With consistent practice, your focus will grow stronger.
  • When your timer brings you to the end of your practice, take a moment of gratitude for showing up for yourself today.
