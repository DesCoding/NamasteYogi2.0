NamasteYogi
Meditation Yoga

  • Set a timer to hold space for the duration of your practice.
  • Click play button on song above.
  • Find a comfortable position.
  • Bring your attention to your breath.
  • Inhale, feel your belly rise and ribs expand.
  • Exhale, fully and completely, allow your lungs to empty.
  • Continue this deep belly breathing as you bring awareness to the song playing.
  • When your mind wanders, acknowledge it and bring your focus back to your breath and the song.
  • With consistent practice, your focus will grown stronger.
  • When your timer brings you to the end of your practice, take a moment of gratitude for showing up for yourself today.
